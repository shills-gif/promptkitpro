Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-25

Overview (plain English)

We collect the minimum information required to process payments and deliver your purchase. We do not sell your data, run advertising trackers, or build marketing profiles. Payments are handled by Stripe.

What data we collect

  • Email address — used only to deliver your download link and respond to support requests.
  • Purchase metadata — such as payment status and a transaction/session identifier (via Stripe).
  • Technical logs (minimal) — basic server logs needed for security and debugging (e.g., request timestamps and error traces).

We do not collect browsing history, advertising identifiers, or behavioural analytics.

How payments work

Payments are processed by Stripe Checkout. Card details never touch our servers. Stripe acts as the source of truth for payment status and sends us a signed confirmation (webhook) so we can fulfil your order.

How we use your data

  • To create and confirm your order
  • To deliver a secure, tokenised download link
  • To provide customer support if you contact us
  • To prevent fraud and abuse, and to keep the service reliable

Data retention

We retain order records only as long as necessary for fulfilment, basic accounting, and legal obligations. Support emails are retained only as long as needed to resolve your request.

Cookies & analytics

This site does not use third-party advertising trackers. Stripe may set cookies that are necessary for secure payment processing on Stripe-hosted checkout pages.

Your rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal data (where applicable) by contacting us. We’ll respond as quickly as practical.
